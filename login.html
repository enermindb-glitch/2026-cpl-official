Sign In

One login for every account type — players, team contacts, referees, and CPL officials all sign in here with the email and password set at registration. You'll land on My Profile, where you can view your card/QR, edit your bio and photo (players), and see your status even while still pending approval.

Not sure which profile is yours? Browse the players directory and open your profile — the edit form appears automatically once you're signed in.

League admin? Sign in to the admin dashboard.
